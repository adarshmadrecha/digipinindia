About DIGIPIN Visualizer

This interactive map shows India's DIGIPIN system - a hierarchical 10-level addressing grid that provides unique codes for every 4m √ó 4m area in India.

How to Use:

  • Zoom: Use mouse wheel or zoom controls to explore different grid levels
  • Hover: Move your mouse over grid cells to see DIGIPIN codes
  • Navigate: Pan around to explore different regions of India
